About us " A life lived for others is a life well lived"
The Barkat welfare society was established by Dr. Randeep Singh Kohli along with his sister Pushmeet Kaur Kohli in the year 2014. Barkat stands for upliftment and the betterment of the society, driven by a passion to serve the society. We aim to provide education, healthcare, and community development to the underprivileged population across India. We've worked for a lot of social cause be it distributing clothes to the underprivileged, installing reflection radiators on the road for Road safety, raising funds for the people in need. The noble deeds of Barkat have been recognized and appreciated by the International Conference and Human library.
Our Aim
Our work aims to break the vicious cycle of poverty and social isolation and to restore hope for a better future. We believe that every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of our society. We also believe that we should provide our environment in return of what we take, so we are aiming to plant 10,000 plants in one month in our current drive and will be contributing to the environment ahead in future in whatever way we can.

Our Objectives
  1. Help in providing medical assistance and legal aid.
  2. To identify the homeless and the destitute and facilitate their relocation in appropriate institutions.
  3. To provide medical and legal aid and assistance to the old aged and to take such other measures to ensure their social well being.
  4. To empower the differently abled by providing: Medical aid and appliances Vocational training and employment opportunities Legal aid Other rehabilitation measures those are necessary to ensure them a self sustained living
  5. To mentor and provide guidance to the youth
  6. To preserve and protect the environment and propagate the significance of sustainable development for maintaining ecological balance.
  7. To conduct study on various social issues from time to time, gather data, and carry out research.
